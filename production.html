FARM

Farms produce food for residents of you homestead. Every 6 hour is produce food for 10 residents (5 pcs. food). Every resident consume 1 food every 12 hour. Upgrade is on 25 residents. Double production will produce two time more, but only one day, after boost will end your residents on 3 day vacation. Every 6 hour you are able to collect your supplies, but if you do that, production will be stop until day end. The farm can be demolished, after demolis you will recieve 10 golds.
